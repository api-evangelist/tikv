Today, the Cloud Native Computing Foundation’s (CNCF) Technical Oversight Committee (TOC) voted to accept TiKV as an incubation-level hosted project. The full announcement can be found on the CNCF blog, and you can find us listed under the CNCF Incubating Projects. We’d like to thank all of our contributors, users, and advocates over the last several years for helping us reach this very important milestone!
