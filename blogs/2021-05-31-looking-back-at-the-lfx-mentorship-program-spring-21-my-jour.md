I am a graduate student in the department of computer science at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany. This spring (2021), I had the pleasure to contribute a major part of the new coprocessor framework to TiKV under the LFX Mentorship Program together with my mentors Andy Lok and Alex Chi.
LFX Mentorship Program logo Background Before I applied to the LFX Mentorship Program, I had only contributed minor stuff to various open-source projects in my free time, like enhancing the documentation, fixing some small issues, and so on.
